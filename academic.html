Smart Swans

Smart Swans program is a extra tutorial for the junior students

We are dedicated to help the junior students who wants to have an improvement in studying. The tutors are came from Year 12 and Year 13 students. We will have the peer-tutoring every week. This is an opportunity for the senior student to gain an experience of teaching, and an opportunity for the junior student to achieve their academic goal.

If you are interested in teaching the junior students or If you need help in studying, please fill in the form below and send it to " westlakegirlssmartswans@gmail.com"
