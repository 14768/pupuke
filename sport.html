  • 9 Aug 2017

    At the Auckland College Sport Individual Swimming Championships held in early August, two students came away with a number of medals. Jelani Wetzell (Year 10) won the following in the Girls 13 and Under Age group: 50m Freestyle (Gold), 50m Backstroke (Silver), 100m Freestyle (Silver). Chloe Seaman (Year 9) won the following in the Girls 13 and Under Age group: 50m Backstroke (Gold), 100m Backstroke (Gold), 50m Freestyle (Silver), 100m Medley (Silver).

  • 5 Jul 2017

    Sophie Egnot-Johnson (Year 12) was part of the New Zealand under-21 Rowing Team that competed at the Under-21 Trans-Tasman Rowing Regatta held in Australia at the beginning of July. Sophie had an extremely successful regatta winning 3 Gold medals. They were gained in the Lightweight Single Sculls, Mixed Lightweight Double Sculls and Mixed Quad Sculls. Sophie was the only New Zealand rower to win all of their races.
